Terms and Conditions

  1. To place an order you have to register on our website.
  2. Once you place your order online, you will receive confirmation by email.
  3. A standard delivery fee of R350.00 will be included, depending on location.
  4. Cancellation of orders less than 24 hours prior to delivery will result in full payment of the order to be made.
  5. All prices quoted inclusive of VAT.
  6. Customer details separate from card details: Customer details will be stored by Tasty Deli (Pty) Ltd separately from card details which are entered by the client on Pay Gate’s secure site. For more detail on Pay Gate refer to www.paygate.co.za
  7. REFUND POLICY Pre-paid orders cancelled 24 hours prior to delivery will be refunded in full.
  8. Refunds will be made via EFT to the client within 5 working days of receiving the cancellation request.
  9. No refunds will be issued for cancellations received on the day of scheduled delivery nor will refunds be issued for cancellations received less than 24 hours before delivery is scheduled.
  10. No refunds will be issued for goods received and signed for.
